Scout

Scout is a Farcaster reader designed natively for iOS, delivering an RSS-like experience with simplicity at its core.

iOS

The easiest way to follow your favorite casters. Never miss a cast. And you never need a wallet.

  1. Farcaster is a sufficiently decentralized social network. Download Warpcast for the full Farcaster experience.

Scout makes it easy to follow Farcasters by username or FID. Stay organized by marking casts as read/unread.

  1. Tap the + button to add a Farcaster.
  2. Dive into individual user feeds or view a combined All Casts feed.

Stay on top of conversations with offline support, saved casts, and app search, all in a native Swift app.

  1. Move between Saved, Unread, and All Casts feeds quickly.
  2. Swipe left to save a cast. Swipe right to mark a cast as read, or quickly mark all casts as read.
  3. Tap the cast to open the Warpcast webview.

Tailor Scout to your needs with filters to hide replies, recasts, or posts with images.

  1. Toggle the badge display to keep track of unread content directly on your app icon.
  2. Enjoy effortless app navigation with launch screen defaults and an option to clear saved casts while keeping your casters intact.
