Farcaster RSS-Style Reader

The easiest way to follow your favorite Farcasters.

  • Follow your favorite Farcasters.

    Scout lets you follow specific Farcaster users by username or FID. Organize and manage their casts with powerful features like marking casts as read/unread, starring favorites, and filtering content to suit your needs.

  • Built for productivity.

    With features like offline support, fast local caching, pull-to-refresh for the latest casts, and search functionality, Scout is your perfect companion for staying updated on Farcaster conversations.

  • Customizable for you.

    Scout offers filters to hide replies, recasts, media-only posts, or URL-less posts. You can even choose between the menu or an all-casts feed as your default view, and load up to 50 historical casts from the last 30 days when adding new users.

  • Powered by SwiftUI.

    Scout leverages the latest iOS technologies, with a sleek and responsive UI built using SwiftUI. It supports dynamic type, dark mode, and Farcaster's signature purple (#8A63D2).
