Scout

Scout is a simple, native RSS-like Farcaster reader for iOS. The easiest way to follow your favorite casters. No account needed.

iOS

Follow Your Favorites

  1. Scout makes it easy to follow Farcasters by username or FID. Stay organized by marking casts as read/unread.

Built for Productivity

  1. Stay on top of conversations with offline support, saved casts, and app search, all in a native Swift app.

Fully Customizable

  1. Tailor Scout to your needs with filters to hide replies, recasts, or media-only posts.

Privacy Policy

  1. Scout does not collect any data.
